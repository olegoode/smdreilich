Sarah Dreilich is a senior at Florida State University, where she pursues a double B.A. in Editing, Writing, and Media and Studio Art and a 12-credit-hour Graduate Certificate in Publishing and Editing. Sarah has completed a range of work throughout her years in Tallahassee, from interning at a local art gallery to freelance copyediting, proofreading, and developmental editing. Sarah has an intimacy with both the artistic and editorial world, with a high interest in the visuality of texts.

Sarah is also the literary coordinator of the board of directors at 621 Gallery in Tallahassee’s Railroad Square art park. She organizes a monthly reading series that features the work of local, emerging writers.

With her intimate knowledge of communicating with authors, attention to detail, artistic background, and overall love for books, Sarah has a unique skill set to bring to the realm of editing and publishing.
